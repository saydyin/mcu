The Forbidden Legacy

— Established For The University —

"We, the members of this University, in order to maintain a structured, harmonious, and disciplined environment, do hereby establish this Forbidden Legacy. All members, regardless of rank—be they Demons, Berserkers, Tormentors, or Mephistopheles—are bound by these sacred Decrees to uphold mutual respect, personal accountability, and the integrity of our institution."

"The dominion of this Forbidden Legacy shall extend across the Main Chamber, the Sinnercells, the Tormentors, and the Berserkers' Chamber."

DECREE I: CODE OF CONDUCT AND DISCIPLINE

01

Section 1. Standards of Manners.

All members, regardless of position, shall maintain composure during conflicts and demonstrate respectful, responsible, and self-controlled behavior at all times.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level, Tormentors and Mephistopheles: First offense would be a warning issued in the Judgement Chamber, where the member will be called to explain their actions. Failure to show understanding or a repeated offense will lead to a final hearing, where a decision of temporary suspension or expulsion may be made based on the severity of the conduct.

02

Section 2. Identification Protocols.

All members must wear their identification badges at all times. Failure to do so will result in a first warning; repeated offenses will lead to expulsion.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: Warning; Expulsion

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Warning; Termination

03

Section 3. Formalities of Address.

High-ranking officials, specifically Tormentors and Mephistopheles, must be addressed formally by their titles, "Ms." or "Mr." followed by their surname; alternatively, they may also be addressed as "Ms." or "Mr." followed by their first name.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: First offense, Warning; second offense, 500 deduction points

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Warning; formal reprimand.

04

Section 4. Prohibition of Obscenity.

Profanity is strictly forbidden during weekdays. However, if the profanity is fully censored or completely concealed with no visible traces, it shall not be considered as violation. Any profanity that remains visible, partially uncensored, or still readable shall be counted as a violation.

Sexual language and R18+ content are prohibited without exception.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: 2000 deduction points

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Sign + 2 recruits

05

Section 5. Public Decorum.

Inappropriate behavior and public displays of affection are strictly prohibited within the university.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: 2000 deduction points

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Sign + 2 recruits

DECREE II: HIERARCHY AND ADMINISTRATIVE AUTHORITY

01

Section 1. Duty of Participation.

Every member is constitutionally obligated to fulfill their assigned tasks and actively participate in their designated roles.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: (Varies according to the discretion of the Sentinel)

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Sign + Recruit; Termination

02

Section 2. Administrative Control.

The power to alter nicknames, group imagery, themes, and institutional settings is reserved exclusively for Mephistopheles, Tormentors, and Berserkers. Unauthorized changes will result in penalties.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level (excluding berserkers): 500 deduction points

03

Section 3. Membership Management.

Only Mephistopheles, Tormentors, and Berserkers possess the authority to add or remove members. Formal removals shall occur on the days when an individual reaches the three-day inactivity.

Inactivity: Members inactive for more than two days without valid justification shall be removed.

Hiatus Violations: Failure to return on the specified date following a hiatus results in automatic expulsion.

Re-entry: Members who depart voluntarily are barred from rejoining for 14 days; violation of this period results in rejection.

DECREE III: COMMUNICATION INTEGRITY

01

Section 1. Transmission Permanence.

The "unsending" of messages within any group registry is strictly prohibited unless they contain real-world information. Editing is permitted, provided it is not used to mask sexual undertones; such deception shall not be tolerated.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: 500 deduction points

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Sign + 2 recruits

02

Section 2. The Decree of Silence (Chat Off).

The invocation of "Chat Off" may only be declared by a Mephistopheles, Tormentors or Berserkers. Once enforced, all must fall silent—no exceptions. Permission to Chat (PTC) is strictly forbidden. No utterance, no message—unless sanctioned by the authority who silenced the space. Only when given leave may questions or complaints be voiced.

Note for Berserkers, Tormentors and Mephistopheles only: Only the one who initiated the "Chat Off" holds the right to lift it. Any interference—such as turning the chat back on without rightful authority—shall be considered a violation. Respect among the ranks is non-negotiable; do not override your peer's silence with your own voice.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: 500 deduction points

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Sign + 2 recruits

03

Section 3. Anti-Spam Measures.

The excessive use of likes, emojis, stickers, or repeating typos more than four times is prohibited.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: 500 deduction points

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Sign + 2 recruits

DECREE IV: INSTITUTIONAL INTEGRITY AND LOYALTY

01

Section 1. Conflicts of Interest.

Romantic relationships between authority figures (Tormentors/Mephistopheles) and demons are strictly prohibited to prevent favoritism, bias, or manipulation.

The following relationships are not tolerated:

  • Mephistopheles & Tormentors
  • Mephistopheles & Demons
  • Tormentors & Demons

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: Expulsion

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Termination

02

Section 2. Exclusivity and Espionage.

The creation of multiple or shared accounts is forbidden. Members must dedicate themselves to this University exclusively; participating in multiple institutions to facilitate espionage is strictly prohibited. Members are encouraged to visit any Sinnercell during weekends, but espionage activities will not be tolerated. Those under hiatus are barred from such visitations.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: Warning; Expulsion

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Warning; Termination

03

Section 3. Sinnercell Stability.

Changing of Sinnercell is strictly prohibited to maintain structure, accountability, and inter-group harmony. For returning members, transference to another Sinnercell shall remain forbidden. Only upon the reopening of the university may they be permitted to transfer.

Exemptions may be granted under the following validated conditions:

  • Transfer due to long-term inactivity of the assigned cell or its leader.
  • Presence of irreconcilable conflicts or proven incompatibility that affect the mental well-being or effective participation of a member.
  • Administrative reshuffling authorized by the Mephistopheles.
Note: "Feeling out of place" may be considered a valid reason only when substantial efforts to resolve the issue have failed and the misalignment proves disruptive to participation or personal well-being. Requests must undergo review and approval by the overseeing authority.
04

Section 4. Confidentiality.

Only authorized information about the university may be shared publicly. Unauthorized disclosures will be penalized.

Judgement

Sinnercell Level: Warning; Expulsion

Tormentors and Mephistopheles: Warning; Termination

DECREE V: PENAL CODES AND "SIGNS"

01

Section 1. Fairness and Reporting.

Fairness is a core value; prohibiting any form of favouritism to ensure equal opportunities and a harmonious environment. Members must report issues of unfairness to the Mephistopheles.

02

Section 2. The Marks of Violation.

• This sign "☒" is added to the nickname of a Mephistopheles or Tormentor for duty negligence

• This sign "⊝" is added to the nickname of somebody who will file a hiatus form.

• This sign "⨷" is added to the nickname of a Mephistopheles and Tormentor who violate a decree without prior warning.

Each mark excluding the hiatus sign requires the recruitment of two (2) new members within 48 hours as a penalty. Failure to meet this requirement will result in the permanent retention of the mark. Accumulating three (3) violation marks will lead to immediate expulsion from the university.

03

Section 3. The Administration of Signs

Placement of Signs: All disciplinary signs or marks shall be appended exclusively to the member's nickname within the Sinnercell group chats.

Mandatory Seclusion: Any member who receives a mark of violation or hiatus shall be automatically removed from the main group chat.

Conditions of Reinstatement: Access to the main group chat shall be restored only upon the official lifting of the mark, following the fulfillment of all restorative penalties or recruitment quotas.
